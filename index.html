About Showrov

I start with the problem, not the interface.

I'm Showrov — a UI/UX designer at EchoLogyx. I care as much about why a product exists as how it looks, so I spend most of my time on research and strategy before I touch the UI.

Every good product I've worked on started the same way: with a real user pain point, not a screen. I design with human-centered methods, a bit of cognitive psychology, and AI in my daily workflow — but the goal never changes. Understand the person, respect their attention, and make it work for the business too.

Design Philosophy

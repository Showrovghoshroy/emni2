About Showrov

Every successful product starts with understanding people.

Showrov Ghosh Roy is a Business-Driven UI/UX Designer passionate about creating meaningful digital experiences through research, strategy, and thoughtful interaction design.

He believes that every successful product starts with understanding users' pain points before designing interfaces. His design philosophy combines human-centered design, business goals, cognitive psychology, AI-assisted design, accessibility, and design for well-being.

Design Philosophy

Design is about solving problems — not decorating screens.

Pain points come first

Understand the user's real problem before opening a design tool.

Business meets user

Business success and user satisfaction should go hand in hand.

Reduce cognitive load

Thoughtful interfaces let people think about their goals, not the UI.

Design for daily life

Build products that genuinely improve people's everyday routines.

Simplicity creates confidence

Clear, calm interfaces make users feel capable and in control.

Good UX is invisible

When design works, nobody notices it — they just get things done.
